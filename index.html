As a Software Engineer at Ancestry, I am involved in the Personal Discoveries project which focuses on the collection and processing of users phenotypic data. In a short span of about 2 years, we have already collected about 60 million phenotypic data from Ancestry's users. These phenotypic data currently powers the DNA science research to discover the correlation between the phenotypic data and genotypic data, and study the genetic markers that influence certain human traits.

The DNA world fascinates me and I am interested in applying advanced machine learning algorithms to understand and unravel the stories hidden in our DNA.
